The Power Cement Ltd project delivers reliable, clean energy through Solar, supporting cement production while reducing environmental impact.

## Project Overview

This installation generates 7 MW of electricity from solar energy, providing stable power to Power Cement Ltd's operations.

## Technical Specifications

The system integrates heat recovery boilers and high-efficiency turbines designed for stable, continuous operation within the plant's process cycle.

## Environmental Impact

By utilizing solar energy the project offsets a significant amount of CO₂ emissions and reduces grid demand.

## Community Benefits

Stable on-site generation strengthens local energy reliability and supports jobs through ongoing operations and maintenance.
