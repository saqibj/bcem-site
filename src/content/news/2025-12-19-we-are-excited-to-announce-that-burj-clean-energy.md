We are excited to announce that Burj Clean Energy Modaraba has signed an MOU with Voltaic Power (Pvt.) Ltd. to collaborate on future commercial and industrial solar projects. Voltaic Power’s cutting edge solar systems make them a promising partner as we work together to provide reliable, sustainable energy solutions. This partnership enables a clean, cost-effective energy solution, aligning with our mission to drive the transition to a greener future.

The agreement was signed by Abdus Samad, COO at Voltaic Power, and Talha Ameer Khan, CFA, Managing Director – Investments at Burj Clean Energy Modaraba.
