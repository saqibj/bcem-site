We are proud to share a major achievement in our partnership with Power Cement Limited. The successful construction of base foundation of Wind Turbine, stands as a testament to the strength of collaboration and technical excellence.

As the end-to-end developer and financier of this project, Burj Clean Energy Modaraba is committed to delivering impactful, Shariah-compliant renewable energy solutions that support industrial decarbonization and long-term sustainability.

This milestone was celebrated on-site in the presence of key leadership from both organizations. Representing Power Cement were, the COO Ahsan Anis and Head of Strategy Department badar bin anwer, while from our side, CEO Nabeel Malik, MD Investments Talha Ameer Khan, CFA, and Head of Technical Wahab Ali were present to mark the occasion. In a symbolic gesture underscoring our commitment to green progress, our CEO also planted a tree on-site, a small act with a powerful message.

With each step, we reaffirm our mission to empower industries through clean energy and contribute to a greener tomorrow.
