We are happy to announce that Burj Clean Energy Modaraba has signed an MOU with SkyElectric Pvt. Ltd, a leader in smart solar energy solutions. As our trusted partner for future projects, SkyElectric’s advanced solar systems will complement BCEM’s efforts in delivering sustainable energy solutions. Their innovative technology and smart energy management, aligns perfectly with our vision for a greener, energy efficient future. We look forward to collaborating with SkyElectric to provide impactful solar solutions that empower businesses across the region.

The agreement was signed by Saad Farhan, Business Unit Head (South) at SkyElectric, and Talha Ameer Khan, CFA, Managing Director – Investments at Burj Clean Energy Modaraba, along with Owais Siddiqui, Strategic Account Manager at SkyElectric, and Wasey Zubair, Analyst at Burj Clean Energy Modaraba.
