Burj Clean Energy Modaraba (BCEM) is pleased to announce record-breaking clean energy generation figures for the fourth quarter of 2024, marking a significant milestone in the company's mission to provide sustainable, Shariah-compliant energy solutions.

## Key Highlights

The quarter saw exceptional performance across all operational projects, with total generation capacity increasing substantially compared to the same period last year. This growth reflects our continued commitment to expanding Pakistan's renewable energy infrastructure.

## Impact on Sustainability Goals

Our wind and solar projects continue to contribute meaningfully to Pakistan's national renewable energy targets, helping reduce carbon emissions while providing reliable, clean electricity to the national grid.

## Looking Ahead

As we enter 2025, BCEM remains focused on expanding our portfolio of renewable energy assets while maintaining the highest standards of Shariah compliance and corporate governance.
