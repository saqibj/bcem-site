We are delighted to share that Burj Clean Energy Modaraba has entered into an MoU with ACT Engineering Services Pvt Ltd. to jointly pursue upcoming commercial and industrial solar projects. With ACT Engineering’s strong technical capabilities and innovative approach to solar solutions, this collaboration positions both organizations to deliver dependable, sustainable, and economically viable energy solutions.

This partnership reflects our mutual commitment to expanding clean energy adoption and contributing to Pakistan’s transition toward a more sustainable and environmentally responsible future.

The agreement was signed by Fida Muhammad Khan, General Manager at ACT Engineering Services Pvt Ltd. and Talha Ameer Khan, CFA, Managing Director – Investments at Burj Clean Energy Modaraba, along with Sameer Ahmed Siddiqui, Shahmir Rizvi and Rajah Mishaz Khan.
